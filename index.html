About Me

One of the greatest challenges of today’s world is finding an environment that feels more like a community and creates mutual dedication, trust, and commitment. Our world has changed dramatically not only over the last few generations, but even more over the last year. I honestly believe we are being challenged to be the greatest versions of ourselves with the capacity to aid others as we wish to be helped.

I seek to apply not only the skills I’ve gained in my study of full-stack web development, but also my experience in fields such as retail, business management, finance, and leadership. I desire to help others attain greater things personally and professionally and to build things that enhance the experience and value of every day life.

One of my greatest strengths is my desire to learn and apply all I can from all different sources. I seek to disrupt complacency and build on my skills. Everyone has something to teach you, it is your responsibility to learn what you can from those around you and to teach what you know to the world.
